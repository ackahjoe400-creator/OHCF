About Us

Oguaa Hall Christian Fellowship is a non-denominational fellowship which is committed to providing Christian values, discipleship, and Christ-like character to the lovely affiliates of the Hall.

Our mission is to foster a vibrant community of believers who are dedicated to knowing Christ deeply and making Him known to others. Through prayer, worship, biblical study, and fellowship, we aim to develop spiritually mature individuals who exemplify Christ's love and character in every aspect of their lives.

We welcome all members of our hall to join us as we grow together in faith and service to our Savior.
